Welcome to Glass Ledger. We are committed to protecting your global financial privacy. This policy outlines our data practices in compliance with Google Play Developer Policies, GDPR, and international privacy standards.

1. SMS Permission & Automated Tracking

To provide seamless, automated expense tracking, Glass Ledger requests READ_SMS and RECEIVE_SMS permissions.

  • Scope: We only process messages from financial institutions (Banks, Cards, Digital Wallets).
  • Local-First Processing: All SMS parsing is performed locally on your device. Raw SMS data is never uploaded to our servers or any third party.

2. AI Brain & Intelligence (OpenAI)

For users subscribed to the AI Brain tier, we utilize OpenAI technologies to provide financial coaching and categorization.

  • Data Anonymization: Only stripped transaction descriptions (e.g., "Grocery Store Purchase") are sent for AI processing. No personally identifiable information (PII) such as bank account numbers or names is ever sent to OpenAI.
  • Security: Data sent to OpenAI is encrypted and is not used to train global AI models.

3. Subscription & Analytics

We use RevenueCat to manage premium access (Ledger Foundation and AI Brain). RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier to manage your digital receipts and ensure your cross-device access remains active.

4. Zero Data Selling Policy

We operate on a simple principle: Your data is not our product. We do not sell, trade, or rent your financial history to advertisers, data brokers, or any third-party entities.

5. Global Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Regardless of your location, you have the right to access, export, or delete your data. Since your data is stored locally, deleting the application or using the "Clear All Data" feature in settings permanently removes all records from your device.

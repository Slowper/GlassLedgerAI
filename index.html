Glass Ledger

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

Welcome to Glass Ledger. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data in accordance with Google Play Developer Policies.

1. SMS Permission & Data Access

Glass Ledger is a financial management tool. To provide automated expense tracking, we request READ_SMS and RECEIVE_SMS permissions.

  • Usage: We only access SMS messages from financial institutions (Banks, Credit Cards, UPI).
  • Purpose: To automatically parse transaction amounts and categories into your local ledger.
  • Privacy: We do not read personal messages. All SMS parsing happens locally on your device. We do not upload your raw SMS data to any server.

2. Data Collection

We collect minimal data to provide our services:

  • Financial Data: Stored locally in an encrypted database on your device.
  • Subscription Data: We use RevenueCat to manage your ₹99 Ledger Foundation and ₹30 AI Brain plans. This involves sharing an anonymous User ID with RevenueCat to verify your purchase status.
  • AI Features: If you subscribe to the AI Brain, transaction descriptions are anonymized and processed to provide financial coaching.

3. Data Sharing

We NEVER sell your data. We do not share your financial information with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your financial data is yours. If you delete the app, your local database is removed. You can also manually clear your ledger at any time within the app settings.
